About CZANE BEAUTY AND ACADEMY

CZANE BEAUTY AND ACADEMY is dedicated to empowering individuals with comprehensive beauty skills. We provide top-tier education in nail technology, make-up artistry, and other beauty disciplines, ensuring our students gain confidence and self-pride through practical and theoretical training. We serve with love, pride, and consistency, always putting our students first, even offering house calls for those with busy schedules.

Our academy, based in Vosloorus Eastfield, began its journey in 2019, with the company officially registered in 2016. It is proudly owned by Zanele Masondo, a visionary Director and 100% South African Woman, whose extensive knowledge, qualifications, and experience in the beauty industry have been the driving force behind the academy's success.

Our Vision

It is our vision to be a leading academy where everyone feels comfortable and welcome, fostering an environment conducive to immersive learning and professional growth. Our goal is that every student receives outstanding education and unwavering support from the moment they enroll until they achieve their professional dreams.

Our Mission

To serve our students with integrity by being loyal and transparent, providing consistent and high-quality training with a happy and relentless attitude, all while maintaining an affordable budget for aspiring beauty professionals and their families.
