Our Services

Acrylic Nails

Artificial nails or nail extensions placed over fingernails as fashion. We use powder and monomer to build them, or alternatively glue and dipping on the powder. Can also be applied to natural nails without extension. These are the strongest nails by far, requiring regular upkeep every two weeks, but can last up to about one month.

Gel Polish Nails

Formed using build-up gel/base coat, Gel Polish, and top coat to get shiny nails. Fancy nails for special occasions, increasingly popular for manicures. We use quality products so that polish stays intact on the nails for at least a couple of weeks with shine and no cracking, peeling, or chipping. The process typically ends with the application of cuticle oil to all fingers to bring nourishment back into cuticles after harsh alcohol application.

Pedicure

Our pedicure mostly includes relaxation, massage, treatment of feet and toenails. Dead skin cells are rubbed off the bottom of the feet using a rough stone. We use sanitized tools for each client, like all our services, and the process ends with massage, cuticle oil, nail polish, and moisturizing.

Make-Up

Make-up for all occasions: Day Make-up, Evening Make-up, Glamour, Photography, Bridal, and any other occasion. Our make-up comes with strip lashes and is applied professionally, ensuring a flawless look made possible by the best face make-up artist in our studio.

Eyelash Extension

Our application is used to enhance the length, curliness, fullness, and thickness of natural eyelashes. We do individual lashes, where one lash is applied to each natural lash to create volume. We have different sizes to fit various natural lashes. We use quality products as eyes are sensitive. After each application, our clients receive a free instruction booklet and care mascara, applied professionally and hygienically by our Extentionist.

Massage

Indulge in our relaxing massage services designed to soothe your body and mind.

About CZANE Nails & Beauty Studio

Czane is a beauty studio mainly focused on the specific needs of beauty. Our purpose is to make our customers feel more confident, confident in themselves, and self-pride. In doing so, we serve with love, pride, and consistency, so our clients feel more welcome. We also do house calls for clients who have busy schedules, with just a call away, we will be at your doorstep. This makes us unique; we always put our clients first.

Czane Studio is based in Vosloorus Eastfield. The business started in 2019, but the company was registered in 2016. The company is owned by Zanele Moyo, Director and 100% South African Woman. "With the knowledge, qualifications, and experience in the beauty industry, I managed to bring the company into existence."

Our Vision

It is our vision to be a studio where everyone feels comfortable and welcome. Our goal is that every client receives outstanding service from the time they walk in to the time they walk out.

Our Mission

To serve customers with integrity by being loyal and transparent to them, the company ourselves, to give consistently with a happy, relentless attitude while standing within an affordable budget for families.
